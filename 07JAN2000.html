FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE 
FREE Internet Services for businesses of all sizes
Chennai, 7th January 2000
 
Chennai based enmail.com, an Internet start up company, today launched FREE Internet services for businesses of all sizes and mediapoint.enmail.com - a comprehensive website for the media. It will be a forum for people to interact with the media and the media amongst themselves.

Speaking at the launch, Karthik Iyer, said, "We will be the fastest growing Indian Internet web site by the end of this year".

He added that "the site already has one thousand business subscribers, through word-of-mouth publicity and we intend to increase this number to one lakh by the end of this year."

Launching enmail.com, T N Seshan, Former Chief Elections Commissioner said, "The Internet must be de-jargonized and made easily understandable to the common man because after-all it is he who must be enabled to reap the most benefits from it".

N Parameswaran, Senior General Manager, VSNL, Chennai, who launched mediapoint.enmail.com said, "while it took a few decades for the first million telephone subscribers, it has taken less than a couple of years for the first million internet users. This makes it vital for companies to have an Internet presence".

enmail.com offers Internet addresses that enhances brand equity, provides 25 MB of space for each company, with unlimited number of e-mail boxes for company employees. enmail.com has bundled the entire gamut of services that enable a company to get an Internet presence, for FREE.

mediapoint.enmail.com is an effort to bring together media professionals on one virtual platform, to hear their views and more importantly to give the common man a single point access to media professionals.

enmail.com is currently a fully owned subsidiary of OOPS, a pioneer in audio and video conferencing over IP networks. Karthik Iyer, has announced plans to spin off enmail.com into a separate company in the next few months.
