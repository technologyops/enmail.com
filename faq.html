Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What does "enmail" stand for?
    The prefix "en" means "my" in the Tamil language; so enmail means mymail, or as we'd like to say...its' e-mail with personality
  1. What services does enmail provide?
    We provide mail management services for businesses of all sizes. We are indispensable for any company that needs dependable messaging services. 

    Currently our services include domain management, web-based e-mail, POP3 access, SPAM prevention, etc. Some of our upcoming services could be Virus scanning of attachments, unified messaging, policy management, etc.
  1. What's the difference between enmail and other Internet Service Providers?
    We are unique in developing indigenous technology required for large-scale e-mail systems. Our Distributed Cluster Architecture (DC Architecture) ensures that our services are affordable, scaleable, and secure. Having control over the technology gives us the ability to customize the services to suit the specific requirements of our customers.

    Our business focus is to provide e-mail/messaging and related services only.
