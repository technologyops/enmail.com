Founded in March 2000, enmail.com focuses on establishing Internet presence for Small & Medium Enterprises and providing outsourced email services for Portals and Internet Service Providers. 

enmail.com was started with the mandate of de-mystifying the Internet to enable the common man's business. In simple terms, our philosophy is to avoid jargons, buzzwords and hype (except for a few in our brochures!!) in presenting our services to companies such as yours.

Our core competency is in building and managing large scale e-mail systems. Our Indigenously developed DC (Distributed Cluster) Architecture provides scalability and reliability at one-fifth the cost of traditional large scale e-mail systems. 

On the services side, because we control the technology, we are unique in providing the ability to customize our technology to suit your company's unique requirements. We are paranoid about network security, and monitor all our services continuously to ensure trouble free operations on a 24 x 7 basis. We have the unique ability to allow/deny access to specific users for specific services in our network in real-time. Basically, if we suspect spurious activity in the network, we prevent access only to those users instantaneously. This ensures trouble free access to all legitimate users.
