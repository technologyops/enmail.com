ENMAIL.COM ANNOUNCES .exe
Chennai, 10th April 2001
 
enmail.com, an Intel portfolio company today announced the launch of its.exe programme. The enmail eXchange event will bring forth the combined benefits of Microsoft Exchange 2000 and enmail's services for the benefit of small and medium enterprises in the Indian market. This service is a comprehensive digital communication package.

The Dual Advantage
Through the .exe programme, enmail and Microsoft Exchange 2000 Server together offer a twin-edge benefit for the small and medium sized corporate - a complete communication package coupled with benefits from the best of both worlds. Microsoft Exchange Server 2000 takes care of the internal communication of an organization and enmail's outsourced mail management services assists companies by taking care of their external communication.

Microsoft Exchange 2000 Server enables organizations to set-up Intranet mailing systems with web-based mailing services and automates internal workflow of organizations, which is easy to use due to its graphical user interface. Further, it is flexible enough to handle a wide range of connectivity spectrum.

enmail takes care of round the clock safety and security of an organizations' external e-mails. enmail offers customized e-mail service to corporates with a brand identity (user@companyname.com) on the world wide web. enmail prevents spam, scans virus in e-mail attachments and provides privacy. The proactive network monitoring of enmail prevents hackers from disrupting mission critical services and thereby yield high-end secure e-mail services.

enmail has sufficient resources on the Internet to meet the requirement and also has adequate infrastructure in place for recovery services with minimum downtime. Also, enmail periodically analyses network usage to ensure that adequate bandwidth is available and properly utilised to ensure smooth operations.

Mr Karthik Iyer, CEO, enmail.com, states, "In a marketplace where the demands of the digital economy place a heavy premium on the ability to exchange information seamlessly, we offer Indian corporates the best and the latest communication package, which is technology driven. The cost advantage of the product will help small and medium sized corporates to reap the benefits of technology and reach out to markets that beyond geographical boundaries."
