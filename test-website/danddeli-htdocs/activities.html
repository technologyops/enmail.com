Ferro Alloy Production Practices Training Program : We at Danddeli Ferro Pvt Ltd take great pleasure in announcing to you the commencement of a specialized Ferro Alloy Production training programme, which will be conducted at our campus in Dandeli, Karnataka.

Danddeli Ferro has the distinction of being Indias first Ferro Alloy Plant. We have leveraged 60 years of knoweldge acquired in the manufacturing of Managenese based Ferro Alloys as well as knowledge garnered from 50 years of Research and Development, to tailor a cutting edge programm for the wider Ferro Alloy community.

The first of a series of programs will be officially termed as 'a SPECIALISED COURSE ON “FERRO ALLOY PRODUCTION PRACTICES – MANGANESE”. It will be an inhouse program conducted at the Danddeli Ferro campus. The programme will focus on a practical understanding of how Manganese based Ferro alloys are manufactured efficiently in an era of scarce resources & competitive markets, and will cover the following topics:

  • Advanced techniques of Ferro Alloy production.
  • Practical training on the Ferro Alloy production process.
  • Theoretical consideration & basic Chemistry
  • Raw Material selection.
  • An overview of production equipment.

We are scheduled to commence classes from the 15th of September 2012 onwards. Classes will run for 3 full days commencing on a Friday morning and ending on the subsequent Sunday evening, once every month. Classes will typically start at 9am in the mornings and run until 6pm in the evening with a break for lunch.

We will be providing prospective students with accomodation, food, course materials and local transfers during the program. The cost of the program will be all inclusive to the student.
