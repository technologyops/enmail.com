Activities / Conceptualizing and Executing Projects :

The team at Danddeli assist investors by leveraging over 50 years of experiences, to conceptualize projects for the successful production of specialty Ferro alloys. The team engages engineering companies and equipment suppliers to provide cost effective project execution. The significant value addition is the supervision of operation service, where the team at Danddeli undertake the responsibility to achieve production within budgeted norms, and provide a training service to train the operators to sustain production efficiencies.
