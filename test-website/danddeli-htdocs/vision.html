Vision :

"To enhance the quality of life of all stakeholders by creating a niche in the global market as a humane, creative and efficient supplier of speciality
products and services."

Mission :

In order to realize our Vision, our Mission is to :

  • Consistently source & produce at the least possible cost specialty products / services.
  • Continuously meet market requirements and maintain an empathetic relationship with customers & suppliers.
  • Train, motivate & empower every individual to live & work as in a spirit of kinship, ownership & co-operation.
  • Undertake R & D activities to improve the prevalent manufacturing process and develop commercially viable new products.
  • Benchmark the performance of the manufacturing facilities, systems & process to achieve the highest international standards.
  • Meet the social & environmental responsibilities of the regions we operate in.
