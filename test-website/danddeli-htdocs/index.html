About Danddeli :

Danddeli currently wishes to leverage its 50 years of knowledge garnered in the areas of conceptualizing, executing, and operating Ferro Alloy Plants. As well as engaging R&D teams with continuous and innovative process improvements. Danddeli wishes to share this knowledge through a residential Ferro Alloy Training course, and by offering expert services for process improvements, Technology transfers, project conceptualization, & supervision of plant operations.

  • India's first Ferro Alloy Plant, established in 1955 by Shri Lalbhai Patel.
  • Over 40 years of applied Research & Development activities.
  • Introduced Manganese & Silico Manganese Alloys to India.
  • Experts at Extra Low Carbon Ferro Manganese Alloys (Furnace Route).
  • Experts at Extra Low Carbon Silico Manganese Alloys (Furnace Route).
  • Introduced many Noble Ferro Alloys (Alumino Thermic Process).
  • Experts at operating with alternate Process Technologies.
  • Quality and Technology Leader.
  • India’s most innovative and resourceful Ferro Alloy manufacturer.
  • Trained innumerable Ferro Alloy Technologists directly & indirectly who are spread across the country, and are the backbone of the Indian Ferro Alloy Industry today.
  • What can Danddeli do for you.?
  • Teach a Certificate Programme in Ferro Alloy Production Practices.
  • Provide Technology Transfers
  • Conceptualize and overview project execution
  • Undertake R & D for specific projects.
  • Train Teams in operations of furnaces
  • Supervise operations of furnaces during technology transfers/commissioning of plants.
