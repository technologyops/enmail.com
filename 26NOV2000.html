Internet enabling small business
Chennai, 26th November 2000
 
Chennai based enmail.com, announced the launch of commercial Internet e-mail services for businesses. Speaking at the launch, Karthik Iyer, said, "Our mandate is to hide the complexities of technology and Internet enable the common man's business."

He added "Providing world-class technology infrastructure, customer support, and the ability to present the Internet using the common man's language is the highlight of enmail.com"
