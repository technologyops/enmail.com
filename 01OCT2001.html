Wireless e-mail in India:the future is here.
Chennai, 1st October 2001
 
Gemini Communications Limited (Gemini), a total networking solutions provider, and enmail.com Private Limited (enmail), an application service provider (ASP) of email and messaging services, announced today that they have signed a MoU to jointly develop and market customized networking & messaging solutions to varied business customers across the country.

The strategic relationship brings together the technological competencies of both companies - Gemini's nationwide network of Marketing offices on Network based technologies & enmail's unique Messaging Services. enmail is the ideal platform for companies looking at secure messaging with high uptime guarantees.

They also announced today their intent to develop, deliver and market wireless e-mail services for business subscribers across the country. For the first time in India, this would make world-class technology available and affordable to the Indian e-mail user. This landmark venture will now free the Indian Internet user from the shackles of being deskbound. Sending and receiving e-mails will soon become as convenient as using the cell phone. Both companies intend to promote this cutting edge technology in the growing rural segment of Internet users, apart from the obvious urban Internet population. Wireless e-mail services will utilize the wildly popular PDAs (Personal Digital Assistants) as the device of choice, and is expected to be available in the first quarter of 2002.

Mr. Ram Kumar, CEO of Gemini Communications Limited, said "we are extremely excited about this new relationship with enmail, in bringing together the best of networking & communication technologies and services under one roof. This is a wonderful opportunity for both our companies to share each other technology strengths. enmail is a must need for today's Corporate Messaging, as it would save them their cost on expensive software, hardware & manpower. The market response for enmail's Messaging services has been very encouraging and the service is likely to reach 10,000 subscribers before the end of this financial year. This definitely is the beginning of a new era in Indian communication history, and in doing so we are benchmarking the levels of Indian networking technology. This will set the trend for making the label 'Made in India' a very proud, indigenous label.

Mr. Karthik Iyer, CEO of enmail.com, also said "This is a proud moment in our young history. Proud because we as a new company are partnering with an existing established Indian company, rather than a foreign one, in bringing such great innovative technologies to the common man. Thus underlying our core belief that technology is a means to an end, and not the other way around. This is also a proud moment because finally the Indian Internet user is not left behind the technological advances of the western world. Soon you will be able to send and receive email from the palm of your hand, any-time from any-place.

About Gemini Communications
Gemini Communication Ltd, is a 6 year old Network Integration company with offices in 10 locations across India & one in the US. Last year revenues on Networking alone was Rs.21 Crores. Gemini has strategic tie-up's with leading Wireless Manufacturers in the world & has recently set up an R&D facility in Chennai to cater to customized Wireless products. Gemini distributes products of Fluke Networks, Tainet etc., apart from being the Integration Partner of many leading Networking companies. Gemini currently has a staff strength of 134 people.

About enmail.com
Founded in March 2000, enmail.com is an application service provider of outsourced Internet e-mail services. They offer simple, scaleable, and secure services to businesses of all sizes. The company targets the large segment of Indian corporates that have been unable to leverage the advantages of the Internet due to lack of knowledge and/or resources.
